About

Somaiya Vidyavihar

Somaiya Vidyavihar is one of India's premier educational ecosystems, established by the visionary Padmabhushan Shri K.J. Somaiya in 1959. Spread across a sprawling 65-acre campus in Mumbai, it is home to 35+ institutions offering programs from kindergarten to doctoral studies, nurturing over 35,000 students every year.

With a commitment to excellence, innovation, and holistic development, Somaiya Vidyavihar has become a beacon of quality education across disciplines including Engineering, Management, Science, Humanities, Medicine, and more. The campus is a vibrant community where knowledge meets culture, tradition embraces innovation, and students transform into future leaders.

Voices is the official student-led media body of Somaiya Vidyavihar, created for the students, from the students, and by the students. We capture the heartbeat of this vibrant campus through stories, videos, and content that celebrate our community.

From the Dean's Office

Dr. Urmi Thakkar

Dean Student Welfare

"At Somaiya Vidyavihar every student is unique and we ensure that each and every student on our campus gives the best not only to make their future bright but also to be the good citizen of our country and give the best to the society.

The university is known for its excellent infrastructure for teaching, learning, research and eco-friendly lush green campus. Also we have Sports academy with multiple facilities on campus.

We have passion of creating Education for all the students in a way it's freedom for our country. Our main aim is to give good quality modern education including a strong component of culture, inculcation of values, awareness of the environment, physical activities and physical education. We want our students to get good Quality Education and give flexibility in curriculum to choose things based on their interests.

Our students on campus have reached heights along with Education in different areas of Sports till International level, launching ISRO Satellite and various start ups reaching multinational companies.

The office of Dean Students' Welfare works for the holistic and comprehensive development of our students. Here, all the activities are undertaken as a measure to promote ample opportunities to the youth to enhance their skills and talents and give them opportunities at national and international level too. Our aim is that every student has core values of integrity, compassion and respect for others. To unleash the talent of our students, we meticulously plan and promote physical as well as cultural activities round the year which include indoor/ outdoor sports events, youth festivals, clubs, and subject related societies. Besides this, my office also ensures to maintain discipline among our students keeping in view their well-being.

At this juncture, I must thank to our visionary Vice Chancellor Prof. Dr. Prof V N Rajsekaran Pillai , who is always keen and determined to help the students in the best possible way."

Ms. Priyanka Shetty

Assistant Manager – Student Welfare

"Ms. Priyanka Shetty is a committed student welfare professional with a strong passion for supporting student growth and well-being. An alumna of Somaiya Vidyavihar, she completed both her graduation and post-graduation from the institution..."

"Ms. Priyanka Shetty is a committed student welfare professional with a strong passion for supporting student growth and well-being. An alumna of Somaiya Vidyavihar, she completed both her graduation and post-graduation from the institution, and now proudly serves her alma mater in a key role.

Currently, as Assistant Manager – Student Welfare at Somaiya Vidyavihar, she plays an instrumental role in creating a vibrant and supportive campus environment. From organizing events and student engagement activities to conducting stress management and mental well-being initiatives, Priyanka ensures that students are nurtured both academically and emotionally. She is also actively involved in planning and executing internship fairs and other career development initiatives, helping students prepare for life beyond college.

Her dedication, warmth, and student-first approach have made her an integral part of many major events and welfare programs across the campus."
