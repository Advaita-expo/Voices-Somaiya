History of Somaiya Voices

Our Mission

Capturing the heartbeat of our campus

Voices exists to capture the heartbeat of our campus and everything that makes Somaiya feel alive.

We document the moments we celebrate, the stories that quietly unfold in the background, and the experiences that shape us in ways we often realise only much later.

Somaiya is not just an educational institution. It is a family made up of countless voices, each one carrying its own dreams, struggles, triumphs and stories.

And just like every family has a story to tell, this is ours.

Our Foundation

Built on three pillars

Creativity

We believe in the power of creative expression to tell stories that resonate and inspire our community.

Connection

We bridge gaps between students, departments, and communities to create a unified Somaiya experience.

Community

We foster a space where every student finds room to express, to be seen and to be heard.

What We Do

More than a media team

Voices is a space where every student finds room to express, to be seen and to be heard. It is a platform that brings together different perspectives, emotions and journeys to create a shared tapestry of memories.
